The Morning After in Saint Laurent SS20

Tina Tyrell Photographs the Hazy Romance of Waking Up Having Never Really Slept

Everyone knows that Los Angeles light is its own particular performance. It stuns. It’s also faded and leaves you a little lost—awed by dimmed yellows that seem leftover from another time, yet hopeful for the day to come. True to the spirit of the Saint Laurent Summer 20 collection, photographer Tina Tyrell captures the romance of last night’s party as it plays out…well into the morning.

Lounging by the pool or easing into breakfast is a special sort of glamour when you’re wearing evening sequins and squinting at the sun. It’s possible the music’s still playing, the bottles are half-full—a testament to that timeless seduction so natural to Los Angeles, the city that actually doesn’t sleep.
